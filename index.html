Our Story

The pioneers of pav bhaji in Gujarat.

It started in 1968 with one obsession — getting Bombay-style pav bhaji right for the people of Rajkot. Decades later, we're still doing it. The same butter-soaked pav. The same secret masala. But now, with a fusion twist — Black Bhaji, Green Bhaji, Cheese Basket Pulav, and a whole Food Lane full of surprises.

Old soul. New tricks. Always Sonali.
